Historical Places

India Gate

The India Gate is a monument located in India's capital city, New Delhi. Standing tall, proud and majestic, it is a tribute to the brave soldiers who fought for India's freedom. It is a symbol of heroism and a reminder of the country's history. The India Gate is a must-visit for anyone who wants to learn about India's rich heritage.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is a historic monument located in New Delhi. It was built in the 12th century and is one of the most famous and recognizable structures in India. The tower is made of red sandstone and has five different stories, each with unique architecture and carvings. It is a popular tourist attraction for its historical significance and its impressive architecture.

Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple, also known as the Bahá'í House of Worship, is a famous temple located in New Delhi. It is a place of worship for the Bahá'í faith, an international religion with over 3 million followers. The temple is shaped like a lotus flower, which represents purity and beauty. It is a beautiful sight to behold and a must-visit for anyone interested in architecture and religion.

Brihadeeswara Temple

Brihadeeswara Temple is a famous temple located in Tamil Nadu, India. It was built in the 11th century and is a UNESCO World Heritage site. The temple is dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva and is known for its complex architecture and intricate carvings. It is a must-visit for anyone who wants to experience Indian culture and history.
